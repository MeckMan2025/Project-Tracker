Privacy Policy

Everything That's Scrum

Last updated: March 6, 2026

Overview

Everything That's Scrum is a team management app built for FIRST robotics teams. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

Information We Collect

The app collects the following information when you create an account:

  • Username
  • Email address

This information is used solely to provide app functionality such as task assignment, team communication, and user identification within your team.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a hosted database service with row-level security. Data is not shared with third parties.

Third-Party Services

We use Supabase for authentication and data storage. No analytics, advertising, or tracking services are used.

Children's Privacy

This app is intended for use by FIRST robotics team members, which may include users under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information beyond what is necessary for app functionality.

Data Deletion

To request deletion of your account and associated data, contact us at grease454@hotmail.com.

Contact
